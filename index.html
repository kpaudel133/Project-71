Campus Life

The Older Elementary

The Older Elementary is one of the classrooms located inside of the Earth House. This classroom hosts students from 4th to 6th grades.

Summer Camps

The Prairie Hill Summer Camp hosts kids from 18 months to 14 years. They make different levels for different grades and age groups. Sometimes, they might put you up a evel if you are mature enough.

The Younger Elementary

The Younger Elementary is the youngest classroom in the Earth House. It hosts kids from 1st to 3rd grades.

School Activities

Children's Country Fair
The Country Fair

The Country Fair is a event that the school hosts every single year. There are game booths and games to play thoughout the campus. The Little Run on the Prairie takes place right before the country fair starts.

What's New?

  • The Meadow House is a new building at Prairie Hill. It is located near the Farm House and the Prairie House. They made this building so that they could move some of the kids from the Farm House. It was finished in the 2019/2020 school year.
